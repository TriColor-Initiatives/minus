Minus Rules

Game Overview

  • Players: 4 total (You + 3 AI opponents).
  • Cards: Standard 52-card deck.
  • Trump Suit: Default = Spades. Can change after first 5 cards.
  • Claims: Declare number of tricks to win; miss it and score minus.

Game Flow

  1. Deal 5 cards each. Player may change trump (must claim at least 5).
  2. Deal remaining 8 cards (4 + 4) to each player.
  3. Players declare claims; claims lock for round.
  4. Play 13 tricks following strict rules.
  5. Score: meet or exceed claim = tricks won; otherwise minus claim.

Strict Play Rules

  1. Must follow suit if possible.
  2. If you can beat highest card in lead suit, you must do so.
  3. No lead suit? Must play trump; beat highest trump if possible.
  4. No lead suit or trump? Play any card.
  5. Highest lead card wins unless trump is played; then highest trump wins.

AI Difficulty

  • Easy: plays lowest legal card.
  • Medium: aims to win with smallest winning card.
  • Hard: tracks cards and plays strategically.
